    • Fun Reading (Circle of Apollonius). If you’re done watching all your favorite shows and you’re into Euclidean geometry: Here you can find a purely geometric proof for the fact that the set of points P on the plane that have fixed ratio of distances from two fixed points A, B is a circle whenever this ratio is not 1.
