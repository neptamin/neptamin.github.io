Nikolas Eptaminitakis

Welcome to my webpage!

I am a Postdoc at the Institute of Differential Geometry, Leibniz University Hannover. Contact information can be found here.

My research is concerned with inverse problems and geometry, with tools coming from (singular) microlocal analysis. In my thesis I studied the X-ray transform in the setting of asymptotically hyperbolic manifolds, whereas more recently I have been working on inverse problems for hyperbolic equations. I am also interested in interplays between the geodesic X-ray transform on closed manifolds and higher Teichmüller theory.
